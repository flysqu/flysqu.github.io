Laptops!

As of writing this i have three laptops

Macbook Air M2 8GB (Got it in December 2024). I really like this laptop, well yeah i like the laptop but i dont love macos, yet... ill probably never truly love macos not vanilla macos at least. I like what ive done with macos now though but i have like 5 different apps to fix different things that i dont like. I currently use AltTab (Makes the window switcher actually usable for me), MediaMate (More for visuals and to make the notch a bit less annoying to look at), Rectangle (Keyboard window controls), SmoothScroll (To make external mice not be forced to have the same scrolling direction as trackpad), Taskbar (Make the dock more like windows, i dont like the dock, it sucks imo). So yeah i honestly hate vanilla macos, soooooo much is missing, but it is usable and its not impossible to daily drive for me with some additions. I LOVE THE HARDWARE, IT MAKES ALL OF THIS WORTH IT, like it feels so smooth and fast and it never really gets hot, the only time ive felt it slightly warm was when i played "Get to work" for like 4 hours straight. And when i say warm i mean like it was still comfortable to use and not uncomfortable

Microsoft Surface 3 . this laptop was gotten for school, it gets a bit loud and i midly dont like the keyboard but its good enough. i use throttlestop to get it to a bit quiter level but it still isnt the best, but i knew that when i bought it lol. it gets pretty good battery life, around 4 and a half hours of watching twitch (thanks dougdoug for that battery test) update: it's battery life is way too bad for school, like it gets to 15% 2 classes into school, so im considering a macbook right now just for their battery life

Thinkpad T450 . i got this one for like 130$? i think, something like that. i really like this laptop, my main use for it is as a backup if the surface breaks and for messing around with linux. some of the stuff i've done with it is LFS, gentoo and hackintoshing it. also yeah i have uh a lot of stickers on it lmao, i like them :3 i've help back on my surface since im gonna be using it for school lmao
